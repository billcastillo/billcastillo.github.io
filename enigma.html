Colors

Colors shouldn’t be too complicated nor overdramatic. It can be unpretentious yet it shouts your name.

On picking a color scheme, I’ve hoped to accomplish the following:

  1. Create a unique identity
  2. Gain a recognizable brand
  3. Bring about a vivid experience

Typography

The following came upon easily.
I looked for a header font that's simple, easily readable. So I aimed for a bold, geometric, yet a bit rounded/humanist font. I thought Montserrat was the perfect choice.

For the body font, I kept in mind that the target geographic can't afford the time to read a long wall of text--if I were required to write some. A font that's a bit condensed will do. PT Sans satisfied my needs
